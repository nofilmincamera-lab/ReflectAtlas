Composite CDI Score: 71.3 (High Drag)
Weighted aggregate (RCL 40%, PTM 35%, TLD 25%) places NovaBank in the 84th percentile for drag among peer banks. The elevated scores across all three dimensions indicate systemic inefficiency rather than isolated issues. Industry median: 58.2

Component Interdependencies

RCL ↔ PTM Correlation (r = 0.71): High role complexity often stems from manual processes. When technology doesn't support workflows, individual roles compensate by absorbing more tasks and decision points.

PTM ↔ TLD Correlation (r = 0.68): Process-tech misalignment typically occurs when workforce composition lacks sufficient technical enablers (15-series roles) to implement and maintain automation tools.

TLD ↔ RCL Correlation (r = 0.64): Without technical leverage, operational roles must handle greater task variety and complexity, driving up cognitive load per position.

8. Methodological Appendix

Data Corpus: 1,200 job postings collected over 90-day period, deduplicated and preprocessed using natural language processing. Text normalized for part-of-speech tagging and semantic clustering.
O*NET Mapping: Job language mapped to O*NET Tasks, Detailed Work Activities (DWAs), Skills, and Technology Skills using semantic embeddings (cosine similarity > 0.75 threshold). 847 unique DWAs identified across corpus.
