Brand Overview

Still Logic is a business intelligence and data platform company that brings clarity to complex data landscapes. Our visual identity reflects this mission through geometric simplicity, bold color choices, and a design system that emphasizes clarity and precision. The brand language is modern, confident, and accessible—making sophisticated data tools feel approachable and trustworthy.

Color Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

  • Use Primary Red for main CTAs and important actions
  • Apply colors consistently across all touchpoints
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility (WCAG AA minimum)
  • Use neutrals for text and backgrounds
  • Leverage color to create visual hierarchy

✗ Don't

  • Mix colors in ways that reduce legibility
  • Use accent colors for body text
  • Create custom color variants outside this palette
  • Apply gradients to logos or icons
  • Override brand colors with arbitrary choices
